The Journey So Far

How a Curiosity Became a Calling

I didn't start with a business plan. I started with a question: what if I could make people's everyday lives genuinely better?

It began small — doing pedicures for friends, noticing how a simple act of care could change someone's entire mood. Then came food delivery, solving the "what's for dinner" dilemma for busy families. And before I knew it, I was hunting down the most beautiful thrift finds for women who deserved to feel extraordinary without breaking the bank.

Each step of this journey has been a wild, beautiful adventure. I've learned that building something real means showing up consistently, caring deeply, and never treating anyone like "just another customer." Every person who trusts me with their business becomes part of my story.
